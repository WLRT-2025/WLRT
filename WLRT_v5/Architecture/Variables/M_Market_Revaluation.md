# M — Market Revaluation

## Status

Provisional Canonical v0.1

---

## Symbol

M

---

## Name

Market Revaluation

---

## Preliminary Definition

Market Revaluation (M) represents changes in Total System Value caused by external market price movements rather than by redistribution activity.

---

## Purpose

M separates passive value changes from value created through redistribution.

---

## Important Observation

An increase in V caused by M does not necessarily improve the Architectural State or the redistribution capability of the system.
