# V — Total System Value

## Status

Provisional Canonical v0.1

---

## Symbol

V

---

## Name

Total System Value

---

## Preliminary Definition

Total System Value (V) represents the aggregate value of all resources currently belonging to the adaptive system.

V describes the economic magnitude of the system.

It does not describe the architectural quality of the system.

---

## Includes

- active assets;
- reserve assets;
- operational resources;
- long-term resources;
- unrealized appreciation.

---

## Does Not Describe

V does not describe:

- redistribution capability;
- Working Configuration;
- Architectural Configuration;
- structural stability;
- adaptive capacity.

Systems with identical V may possess completely different architectural states.

---

## Purpose

V measures system size.

It does not measure system quality.
