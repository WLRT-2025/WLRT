# CA — Architectural Configuration

## Status

Provisional Canonical v0.1

---

## Symbol

CA

---

## Name

Architectural Configuration

---

## Preliminary Definition

Architectural Configuration (CA) represents the organization of resources responsible for preserving, restoring and developing future Working Configurations.

CA supports architectural continuity rather than operational execution.

---

## Purpose

CA preserves the long-term ability of the system to reproduce redistribution cycles.

---

## Determines

CA influences:

- architectural resilience;
- recovery capability;
- long-term development.

---

## Does Not Describe

CA does not measure:

- current operational readiness;
- current redistribution activity;
- immediate execution capability.

---

## Current Interpretation

CA changes more slowly than CW and represents the long-term architectural state of the system.
