# Research Framework

## Status

Working document.

This document defines the research framework for the architectural branch of Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT) v5.0.

It is intended to organize the development of new concepts before their mathematical formalization and scientific publication.

---

# Scope

This research does not develop commercial rules for BitCapital.

BitCapital serves only as an experimental validation environment.

The purpose of this research is to identify universal architectural principles applicable to adaptive systems operating under the laws of WLRT.

---

# Research Methodology

Every new concept follows the same sequence.

1. Observation

A new phenomenon is observed in practice.

2. Generalization

Determine whether the phenomenon is universal or implementation-specific.

3. Canonical Definitions

Introduce new objects, variables and terminology.

4. Mathematical Formalization

Develop mathematical relationships, invariants and equations.

5. Validation

Verify the model using BitCapital and, where possible, other adaptive systems.

6. Publication

Publish each completed research block independently.

# Relationship to WLRT

Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT) describes universal laws of liquidity redistribution.

The architectural research direction investigates how adaptive systems should be organized in order to operate sustainably under those laws.

This direction extends WLRT without modifying its existing fundamental principles.
