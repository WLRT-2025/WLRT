# State Transition

## Status

Provisional Canonical v0.1

---

## Definition

A State Transition is the transformation of one Architectural State into another following the completion of a redistribution cycle.

---

## General Form

State(n)

↓

Redistribution

↓

State(n+1)

---

## Properties

Every redistribution cycle produces exactly one new architectural state.

A transition may modify:

- Working Configuration;
- Architectural Configuration;
- Total System Value;
- Resource Allocation;
- Redistribution Capability.

---

## Current Research Questions

Current research investigates:

- reversible transitions;
- irreversible transitions;
- stable transitions;
- degrading transitions;
- recovery transitions.

---

## Purpose

State transitions provide the foundation for describing adaptive system dynamics mathematically.
