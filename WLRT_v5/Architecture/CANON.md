# Architecture Canon

## Status

Canonical

---

This directory follows the canonical development sequence adopted for the architectural branch of Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT) v5.0.

The sequence is fixed.

New concepts should be added without violating this order.

---

## Canonical Development Order

1. Research Framework

Defines the scope of research.

---

2. Architectural Objects

Defines the objects of the theory.

---

3. Architectural Relationships

Defines interactions between objects.

---

4. Architectural Principles

Defines structural principles.

---

5. Architectural State

Defines the complete state of an adaptive system.

---

6. Variables

Introduces mathematical variables.

---

7. Mathematical Model

Defines equations and invariants.

---

8. Architectural Laws

Introduces formal laws.

---

9. Validation

Validates theoretical predictions.

---

10. Publications

Publishes validated results.

---

## Rule

Higher levels may depend on lower levels.

Lower levels should never depend on higher levels.

Objects are defined before variables.

Variables are defined before equations.

Equations are defined before laws.

Laws are validated before publication.
