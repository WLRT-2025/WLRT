WLRT — Short Theory Overview

Status: active / core-summary

Description:
This branch provides a concise overview of the
Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT).

Contents include:
- core assumptions of WLRT,
- definition of liquidity fields,
- concept of redistribution and phase transitions,
- role of the Local Fragility Index (LFI),
- scope of applicability and known limitations.

The goal is to give a precise but compact
representation of WLRT for expert readers
without requiring full immersion into the Core.
