WLRT Strategy, Tactics, and Operational Measures

Status: active / meta-strategy

Description:
This branch addresses strategic, tactical, and operational questions
related to the promotion, dissemination, adoption, and practical use
of the Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT).

Scope includes:
- scientific positioning of WLRT,
- publication and communication strategy,
- interaction with academic and practitioner communities,
- responsible use and limitation of WLRT-based insights,
- long-term sustainability of the theory as a research program.
