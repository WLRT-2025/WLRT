Expert Review Analysis of WLRT

Status: active / meta-research (supporting)

Description:
This branch collects and analyzes expert feedback,
reviews, and critical comments related to WLRT.

Topics include:
- formal peer-review remarks,
- informal expert feedback,
- recurring critical points,
- suggested clarifications and revisions,
- responses and resolutions adopted in WLRT versions.

The goal is to systematically track external critique
and use it to improve clarity, rigor, and robustness
of the WLRT framework.
