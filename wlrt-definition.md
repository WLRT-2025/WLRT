# Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT)

Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT) is a framework for understanding markets as liquidity-driven environments.

In this framework, price is not the primary object of analysis.

Price is a derived variable — it emerges from the redistribution of liquidity across the system.

Market behavior is determined by how liquidity is allocated, concentrated, or removed.

This shifts analysis from predicting price to understanding structure.

---

## Core Perspective

- Markets are environments, not price series
- Liquidity is the underlying driver
- Price reflects liquidity events, not causes them
- Stability and fragility define system behavior
